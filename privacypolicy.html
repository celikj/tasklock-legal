Privacy Policy for TaskLock

Last Updated: July 20, 2026

TaskLock ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("TaskLock" or the "App").

1. Information We Collect

A. Data Stored Locally

TaskLock is designed to prioritize your privacy. All your core data—including your tasks, habits, dailies, and Screen Time preferences—is stored locally on your device. We do not sync this personal data to our servers.

B. Screen Time API

TaskLock uses Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls, DeviceActivity, and ManagedSettings) to block distracting apps while you have active locking tasks.

  • We do not have access to the names, icons, or identities of the apps you choose to block.
  • Apple provides us with opaque tokens that we use to enforce the blocks. We never transmit these tokens off your device.

C. Telemetry and Analytics

To improve TaskLock, we collect anonymous usage data (telemetry). This data does not contain personally identifiable information or the contents of your tasks/habits.

  • We track anonymous events (e.g., "Task Completed", "App Blocked", "Subscription Purchased").
  • This helps us understand how the App is used and identify bugs or performance issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide the Service: We use your local data and Screen Time permissions strictly to provide the core functionality of the App (blocking apps until tasks are completed).
  • To Improve the App: Anonymous telemetry helps us prioritize new features and fix bugs.
  • To Process Purchases: If you purchase a premium subscription, payment processing is handled securely by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not have access to your credit card information.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect the limited information we collect. Since your core data remains on your device, it is protected by the security mechanisms of iOS (such as your passcode or Face ID).

4. Third-Party Services

TaskLock utilizes the following third-party services:

  • Apple (App Store & Screen Time): For app distribution, Screen Time API, and subscription billing.
  • RevenueCat: For validating and managing premium subscriptions.
  • TelemetryDeck (or similar): For collecting anonymous usage data.

Please review their respective privacy policies for more details on how they handle your data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@tasklockapp.com.
